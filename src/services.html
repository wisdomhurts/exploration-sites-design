GIS Maps

Publication-ready maps from your geological data.

We produce eight types of GIS maps — from simple locators to complex 3D block models. Every map is built from your raw data using QGIS, styled for investor communications, and delivered print-ready.

Locator Maps
Regional context for property location. Show investors where your project sits relative to infrastructure, cities, and known deposits.
→
Area Play Maps
District-scale geology and land position. Illustrate the broader geological setting and nearby operations.
→
Property Maps
Claim boundaries, infrastructure, and access. Clear delineation of your mineral tenure and key site features.
→
Geological Maps
Lithology, structure, and alteration zones. Detailed geological mapping with custom symbology and legend.
→
Geophysical Maps
Magnetics, EM, and gravity survey data. Visualize your geophysical surveys with professional color ramps and contours.
→
Geochemical Maps
Soil and rock assay contours. Present your geochemical data with interpolated surfaces and anomaly highlights.
→
Cross Sections
Drill collar and interval visualization. Show subsurface geology, mineralization, and drill intercepts in section view.
→
3D Block Models
Resource model and drill hole renders. Communicate your resource estimate with clear, compelling 3D visualizations.
→
