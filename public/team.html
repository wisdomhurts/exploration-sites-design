Our Team

Sector-native. Not outsiders learning as they go.

We speak the language of the rock before we speak the language of design. A tight team with deep expertise in GIS, visualization, and investor communications — of this industry, not servicing it from the outside.

Dorian Leslie
CEO
With over 30 years of experience in the mineral exploration industry, Dorian understands that exploration data can be both technically precise and visually compelling. Today, he leads Exploration Sites, overseeing a multidisciplinary team of GIS specialists, geologists, and designers dedicated to transforming complex exploration data into clear, investor-ready visuals.
Esther Makowichuk
CFO
Esther oversees the financial operations of Exploration Sites, managing budgets, contracts, and financial planning to ensure the business runs as precisely as the maps it produces.

Her hands-on field experience allows her to bridge the gap between technical teams and market-facing content — helping clients communicate their results with clarity, precision, and impact.
